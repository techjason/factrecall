How to remember anthing?

Can you remember every single fact that you have read in a text book? Probably not! When we read through something passively, our brains are not forced to work at all. By by recalling information that we have read using flashcards, questions or exercises, our brains are constantly stimulated to actively recall information, making us easier to store information in our brains for the long run.
